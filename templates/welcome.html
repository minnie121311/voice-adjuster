Voice Perception and Adjustment Study

Study Information

Research Team: University College London (UCL)

Estimated Duration: Approximately 30-40 minutes

Purpose of the Study

This research investigates how people perceive and prefer different voice characteristics in workplace communication scenarios. You will listen to various voice samples from simulated HR department messages and evaluate them.

What Will You Do?

This study consists of several parts:

  • Questionnaire: Brief survey about social interaction (LSAS)
  • Phase 1: Listen to 36 voice samples and rate them
  • Phase 2: Adjust voice parameters to find your preferred voice tone

Important Notes:

  • Please use headphones for the best audio quality
  • Complete the study in a quiet environment
  • Your responses will be completely anonymous
  • You can withdraw at any time without penalty

Data Privacy

All data collected will be anonymized and stored securely. Your responses will be used only for research purposes and will not be shared with third parties.
